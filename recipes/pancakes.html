Pancakes

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Egg

    • Steps

    1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
    2. Add the wet ingredients and stir.
    3. Scoop the smooth batter onto a hot pan.
    4. Cook for two to three minuts and then flip.
    5. Cook on the other side for the same amount of time.
