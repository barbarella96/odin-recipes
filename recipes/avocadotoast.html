Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is an open-face sandwich made with toasted bread topped with smashed (or sliced) avocado and (typically) fresh lemon juice, olive oil, and simple seasonings like salt and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Bread
  • Avocado
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lime
  • Egg

    • Steps

    1. Cut the avocado, remove skin and nut.
    2. Smash the avocado and mix it with salt, pepper and lime juice
    3. Toast the bread and add the freshly made guacamole.
    4. Cook the egg as you prefer.
    5. Add the egg on top of the toast.
