Chocolate Cookies

A chocolate chip cookie is a drop cookie that features chocolate chips or chocolate morsels as its distinguishing ingredient.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Chocolate chips

    • Steps

    1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
    2. Add the wet ingredients and stir.
    3. Add the chocolate chips.
    4. Make a few medium-sized balls with the dough.
    5. Bake in the oven.
